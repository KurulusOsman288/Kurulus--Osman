About the Series

Kuruluş Osman chronicles the epic journey of Osman Bey, the founder of the Ottoman Empire. With rich storytelling, breathtaking battles, and intense drama, it is a historical series that captures the essence of leadership, struggle, and empire building. Follow Osman as he faces both external enemies and internal struggles to establish the Ottoman Empire.
